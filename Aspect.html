ABOUT US

Clients come to Aspect for our consistently innovative approach to flower design. We are committed to offering only the finest floral arrangements and gifts, backed by service that is friendly, prompt and personal.
Aspect incorporates eco-friendly flowers, products, materials, and practices in our studio and in every project we undertake. This is why we also have a seed bank and usually place free packets of wildflowers in bouquets so as to provide our clients with a lasting reminder of the occasion.
